Who are you?

Hello, and welcome to my website! I'm Fif Gallagher, a recent Computer Science graduate from San Francisco State University.

My journey to the tech field wasn't.. obvious. As a kid I wasn't allowed to play video games (my mother predicted I would become addicted to them), and in elementary school I somehow managed to render our blocky desktop computer unresponsive by downloading screensavers. So technology wasn't an obvious career path for me. But somethings I /was/ fascinated by as a kid were adjacent to CS. I remember spending many hours shuffling and re-sorting playing cards, trying to figure out how to make the process involve as little effort from me as possible. The process I landed on was to flip through each card one by one and ask a question of each card within that mini-deck:
    What color is this card?
    Which suit?
    Is it a face card?
    Is it the biggest card?
Realizing many years later that I had independently thought of sorting algorithms without knowing what they were was a real joy. All this to basically say that I was always fascinated by /systems/. Airports, Rube Goldberg machines, those cutaway diagrams with 3d models of life in middle-age castles... in hindsight, all these interests poised me to love the theoretical side of computer science. Making code readable, modular, and scalable are the things I find fascinating. But to me, what's great about computer science is its ability to put complex ideas into a framework not only for discussion, but to make it tangible. That's what I hope to accomplish in my career.
