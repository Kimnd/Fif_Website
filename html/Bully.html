Bully

Time and Tide : An excruciatingly long story about a teenager who is inexplicably sent back to the start of the school year after failing to become the ruler of his school. The teenager, being an asshole, learns to use this to exploit those around him for his own whims. This goes poorly for all involved.
Groundhog Pete : A less excruciatingly long story about a teenager who is inexplicably sent back to the start of the school year after helping somebody become the ruler of their school. The teenager, not being an asshole, tries to change the series of events that lead up to the previous failure. It takes a bit, but he gets there eventually.
